As a member of the eBay Developers Program, you have access to eBay’s platform and technical resources. eBay APIs offer you the power to create a wide variety of solutions to meet the diverse needs of the millions of buyers and sellers on eBay. Using our program resources, you can create your own apps that communicate directly with eBay business logic and databases. Your apps can provide a custom interface, functionality, and specialized operations that are not available through the eBay web flow or mobile applications.
