Join the eBay Partner Network (ePN) to earn money by driving traffic and prompting sales across one of the world's largest and most diverse marketplaces.

## Learn about Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is a referral program where sites pay commissions to affiliates who send traffic to their site that results in a sale. To learn more about our affiliate marketing program, see [What is Affiliate Marketing?](https://partnernetwork.ebay.com/our-program/what-is-affiliate-marketing)

## Join the eBay Partner Network and earn money

You can [learn about the eBay Partner Network](https://partnernetwork.ebay.com/) on its website, [join the program](https://partner.ebay.com/campaign-promo-signup/eBay-Partner-Network.brand), and then discover [how to generate trackable affiliate links](https://partnernetwork.ebay.com/solutions/creating-affiliate-links) that you can share on websites, apps, blogs, or social media networks.

When any of your links results in a qualifying transaction, you earn a percentage of the sale price as outlined in [our rate card](https://partnernetwork.ebay.com/our-program/rate-card).

## Grow traffic and relevancy with Buy APIs

Learn about the integrations that will help you [grow traffic and improve buyer relevancy](https://partnernetwork.ebay.com/solutions/grow-traffic-and-relevancy-with-ebay-s-buy-apis) such as tapping into eBay Buy APIs, scaling listings on your site with data feeds, and learning about your true revenue potential with incrementality testing.

If you want to know how to increase your call limits and access additional APIs, see [Application Growth Check](/grow/application-growth-check).
