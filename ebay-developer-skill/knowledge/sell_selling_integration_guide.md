The eBay Sell APIs are RESTful APIs that use [OAuth authentication](/api-docs/static/oauth-tokens.html), [JSON payloads](/api-docs/static/rest-request-components.html),
and [eBay HTTP headers](/api-docs/static/rest-request-components.html#headers). They provide capabilities needed to create an eBay selling experience in your app or website. See [Selling Appss](/develop/apis/restful-apis/sell-apis#) for available Sell APIs.
