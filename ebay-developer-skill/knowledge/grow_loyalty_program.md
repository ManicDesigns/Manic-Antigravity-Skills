The Loyalty Program recognizes and rewards our best sell-side developers. It has three tiers, which reflect a developer’s eBay business scale and solution sophistication using eBay APIs:

* **Gold** – The top performers
* **Silver** – The up-and-coming
* **Bronze** – The small but promising

The program is currently invite only. Placement in or movement between tiers is made at the sole discretion of eBay.

All developers in good standing are eligible for consideration.

## Badges

Loyalty Program Badges indicate whether a developer is an eBay Gold, Silver, or Bronze Solution Provider.  A developer can use their badge to promote their tier status in accordance with the [API License Agreement](/join/api-license-agreement) and the [Badge Usage Terms](/grow/loyalty-program/badge-usage-terms).
